Welcome to FloodHelp API

FloodHelp API is designed to provide real-time updates about flood conditions in the Brisbane area. Through the use of the api in the FloodHelp mobile app. It allows users to view their location on a map, identify nearby hazards, and track the locations of family members or selected individuals.

Users can report hazards by selecting a location, uploading an image, and adding a title. The app highlights high-risk zones based on the number of hazard reports, indicating areas of potential danger. This crowdsourced information is combined with data from official sources, such as the Brisbane City Council and Queensland Emergency Services, to give you accurate, location-specific flood updates.
